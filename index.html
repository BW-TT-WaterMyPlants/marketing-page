Never Forget To Water Again

We've all been there... We get busy and forget our favorite azalea in the yard. Or that ficus your grandma bought for you. Suddenly, they're all dead. With Water My Plants, you can keep track of all your plants and be reminded when to water them.

frontpage headline

Do you know that watering a plant has a right and wrong way? In this “HOW TO” segment, Denise Chavez from our horticulture department, will walk you through the best way to water your plants in order for them to live up to their full potential.
